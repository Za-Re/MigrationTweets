3. 20 Top entities and hashtags analysis

We first selected top 20 entities and hashtags of whole year 2016. Then they have been ranked and depicted based on their frequencies on a weekly basis. Among top 20 entities, there are 7 entities which do not contain any of our keywords we used to query. For example, Germany and Turkey are two countries and Donald Trump is a politician which co-occurred with our entity and hashtag keywords more often than their counterparts. Germany had the largest number of migrant arrivals and asylum applications during this time period and Turkey as a transit country hosted the largest number of syrian asylum seekers in 2016.
